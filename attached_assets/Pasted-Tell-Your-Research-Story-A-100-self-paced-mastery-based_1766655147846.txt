Tell Your Research Story
A 100% self-paced, mastery-based online platform for graduate-level academic writing skills, built entirely on the works of John M. Swales and Christine B. Feak.
amazon.comamazon.com

The platform draws exclusively from Academic Writing for Graduate Students (3rd ed.), Telling a Research Story: Writing a Literature Review, and their official commentaries/excerpts (as provided in attached PDFs).
Core Design Principles

No live instructors, forums, or peer interaction.
Progression locked until mastery (80% threshold).
All learning active: quizzes, writing tasks, flashcards, mindmaps.
100 interactive quizzes, 100 flipping flashcards (spaced repetition), 40 interactive mindmaps.
Automated feedback from original text snippets and commentaries.

research.commasterytrack.org

Platform Structure

Program: Tell Your Research Story
6 Modules (sequential, mastery-locked):
Foundations of Academic Writing
Problem, Process, and Solution
Data Commentary & General-Specific Texts
Similarity/Contrast, Critiques, Manuscript Reviews
Research Paper Construction (Results, Introductions, Discussions, Abstracts, Acknowledgments)
Literature Reviews (full TRS integration)

Interactive Elements Distribution:
100 quizzes (8–12 questions each, randomized banks)
100 spaced-repetition flashcards (key terms, skeletal phrases, structures)
40 clickable/expandable/rearrangeable mindmaps (e.g., CARS model, problem-solution flow)


noji.iomeistertask.com

Key Features

Short text excerpts (≤500 words) + scripted explanations (≤5 min audio/video if implemented).
Practice: Original writing tasks (e.g., draft Results section, construct literature review paragraph).
Assessment: AI-graded via keyword/structure match to Swales/Feak examples.
Feedback: Immediate, error-specific, with direct PDF commentary redirects.
Motivation: Progress bars, mastery badges, verifiable certificate on 80% overall completion.
Analytics: Track mastery rates, retries, drop-offs for continuous improvement.

This platform replaces traditional instruction with rigorous, automated mastery paths focused on transferable academic writing skills—telling clear, structured research stories.
